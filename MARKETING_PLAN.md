# CourtVision AI: Marketing & Growth Strategy

## 1. Product Positioning
CourtVision AI is the "Moneyball" tool for amateur and semi-professional basketball teams. It bridges the gap between simple scorekeeping and professional-grade tactical analysis.

## 2. Launch Strategy (The "First 1000" Plan)
- **Phase 1: Beta Community (0-100 users)**
  - Reach out to local basketball leagues and high school coaches.
  - Offer a free "Team" plan for the first season in exchange for feedback.
- **Phase 2: Content-Led Growth (100-500 users)**
  - Generate "AI Tactical Breakdowns" of major NBA/EuroLeague games and post them as threads on X (Twitter) and LinkedIn.
  - Use the "Scorekeeper" feature to create viral infographics comparing top players in local leagues.
- **Phase 3: Influencer Partnerships (500-1000 users)**
  - Partner with basketball YouTubers and skills trainers.
  - Provide them with exclusive AI insights they can use in their training sessions.

## 3. SEO Strategy
- **Target Keywords:** "basketball scorekeeper app", "AI basketball analysis", "advanced basketball metrics", "basketball tactical insights".
- **Content Structure:**
  - **Glossary:** Pages explaining advanced metrics (PER, TS%, Net Rating).
  - **Match Previews:** Automated AI previews for every major basketball game.
  - **Player Profiles:** Dynamic pages for top players with AI-generated performance summaries.

## 4. Social Media Strategy
- **X (Twitter):** Real-time tactical threads during live games. Tagging major basketball journalists.
- **TikTok/Reels:** 60-second "AI vs Reality" videos comparing our predictions to actual game results.
- **LinkedIn:** B2B focus on sports management and data science applications.

## 5. Conversion Funnel
1. **Top of Funnel:** Viral social media threads + SEO match previews.
2. **Middle of Funnel:** Free "Fan" plan requiring sign-up. 5 free AI insights/day.
3. **Bottom of Funnel:** "Pro Analyst" plan ($19/mo) or "Team" plan ($49/mo) for unlimited insights and advanced metrics.
4. **Retention:** Weekly "Tactical Digest" emails based on the user's favorite teams.

## 6. Monetization
- **Freemium Model:** High-volume free tier to capture data, premium tier for power users.
- **B2B API:** Future potential to license our analysis engine to smaller sports blogs and betting communities.
- **Team Subscriptions:** Group billing for entire coaching staffs.
